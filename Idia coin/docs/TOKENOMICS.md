## Value Generation Mechanisms

### 1. Staking System

#### Base Staking
- Minimum Stake: 1,000 IDIA
- Base APY: 8%

#### Lock Period Multipliers
- 30 days: 1.2x rewards
- 90 days: 1.5x rewards
- 180 days: 2.0x rewards
- 365 days: 3.0x rewards

### 2. Fee Structure

#### Transaction Fees
- Base Fee: Dynamic based on network usage
- Privacy Premium: +50% for privacy features
- Fee Distribution:
  - 40% to Stakers
  - 30% to Treasury
  - 30% to Privacy Pool

#### Fee Burning
- 3% of all fees are burned
- Reduces total supply over time
- Creates deflationary pressure
